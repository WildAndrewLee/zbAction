In order to keep your zbAction installation as secure as possible. We recommend that you follow the following guidelines:

  • Never share your zbAction password with anybody.
  • Only add modifications you trust.
  • Keep all zbAction modification code below the zbAction code.
  • Don't touch the zbAction code unless you know what you are doing.
  • If you feel an added modification is not safe, remove it from your zbAction account first. After that, remove it from your board template. Removing a modification from your account results in an immediate change. Removing a modification from your board template does not—a page reload is required in order for the change to take effect.
